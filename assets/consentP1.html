Viral Diagnosis

In this online study you will be asked to go through two phases: one learning phase and one testing phase. In the learning phase you will be shown some mild symptoms presented as text, e.g. “tiredness”, and then sort these symptoms into their respective diseases, which are completely fictional. During this phase, you will also be provided feedback on your accuracy. After a certain number of correct trials are done, you will move on to the testing phase. In the testing phase, you will be asked to sort symptoms into diseases without corrective feedback. Your camera will be used to track your eye movements during the study. There will not be any video or audio recording of you.

The purpose of the experiment is to study basic processes of learning. There are no foreseeable risks or discomfort associated with completing this study.
